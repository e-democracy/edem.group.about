Introduction
===========

This product customizes the About Group box of `GroupServer`_, provided by
`gs.group.about`_. Significant customization includes moving the About Group
box from the primary column of the group homepage (provided by 
`edem.group.home`_ and `gs.group.home`_) to the secondary column.
